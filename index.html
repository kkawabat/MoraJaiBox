Mora Jai Box Simulator

Pattern Rules

Enter 13 colors, colors 1-3 determines box colors the top row.

Colors 4-6 determines the box colors of the middle row.

Colors 7-9 determines the box colors of the last row.

Colors 10-13 determines the desired realm color starting from upper-left corner and moving clockwise.

Game Rules

Match the corner colors.
  • grey — Tile acts as an empty space and has no function.
  • black — Moves all of the tiles in the row one to the right.
  • red — Turns all white tiles black and all black tiles red.
  • green — Tile swaps positions with a tile in the opposite position.
  • yellow — Tile moves up one position.
  • pink — Rotates every adjacent tile one clockwise position.
  • violet — Tile moves down one position.
  • orange — Tile changes colors to match the majority of its adjacent tiles. If there adjacent tiles are evenly split, the tile will not change colors.
  • white — Tile expands outwards if there are any adjacent gray tiles. If there are no adjacent gray tiles, the white tile will disappear.
  • blue — Tile will copy the ability of the tile in the middle of the 3x3 grid.
